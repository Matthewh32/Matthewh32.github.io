About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I'm Matthew, a physics graduate with a passion for astronomy, astrophotography, and building software tools for scientific applications. My skills include programming (Python, C++, MATLAB, Git, Linux, frontend languages) and data analysis. I have a strong foundation in research, with experience at Brookhaven National Laboratory as well as coursework and volunteer research at Biola University. I am also an avid astrophotographer, and I enjoy capturing the beauty of the cosmos. This website is a collection of my work, experience, and interests. Feel free to explore and reach out!
